This season seems to be very colorful compared to past season

A kaleidoscopic medley of primary colors and design references debuted at Esteban Cortazar for their Spring 2017 runway show. Inspired by his travels to India and the fluidity of the schoolkids there mixed traditional saris with bomber jackets and sweaters, Cortazar looked to bring their bold hues and distinct draping style into his own collection. Add additional references from R&B muses like Sade, Lauryn Hill, and Erykah Badu, a dash of rastas, and personal connections to locales like Ibiza and South Beach Miami and it’s fair to assume it all to making sense might have been too difficult a task.
